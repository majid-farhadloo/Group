Job Summary:

Assemi Group, Inc. is seeking an experienced, motivated, fast-paced professional to design integrate, organize and manage the changing needs of their business. The Manager of Enterprise Content Management (ECM) Development is responsible for the implementation and operation of ECM systems. This includes but is not limited to training users, work with various departments to identify their ECM requirements, perform system administration, establish and maintain a quality assurance process, and manage SQL database for data imports. This position is also responsible for the direct oversight and development of all staff in the ECM Department, ensuring the department is reaching their goals.

 

Essential Functions/Accountabilities:

o   Understanding the data structures, operation, and application of the ECM implementation for management of business data documentation

o   Primary POC for Help Desk assistance to end users

o   Diagnose ECM issues and recommend solutions

o   Assist users with development of customized views

o   Assist users organize their data into the ECM structures

o   Work with businesses, managers and end-users to identify and document ECM requirements

o   Create and maintain the ECM Data Dictionary

o   Write code, database queries and perform system administration across various servers

o   Verify that logic is appropriate and validate the system operates as designed

o   Assist with development and documentation of user documentation, business process documentation, SOPs, naming conventions, process conventions and workarounds

o   Create and maintain ECM training documentation

o   Development and Maintenance of M-Files Document Templates

o   Assist with establishment of Quality Assurance processes, reviews, auditing, algorithm and test development, and reporting

o   Maintain design documentation including ER database diagrams

o   Audit, modify, and amend data in systems using SQL commands, hand-editing, and bulk import

o   Manage SQL database integration and create ETLs to import data into ECM

o   Experienced in the real estate transactions or development/construction highly preferred
